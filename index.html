ShipMate Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to ShipMate's Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you interact with our services, including but not limited to website usage, mobile application engagement, and customer service interactions.

Information Collection

We collect various types of information when you use our services. This includes information you provide directly, such as personal details when setting up an account, as well as information gathered through automated means like cookies when you interact with our website.

Information We Collect Automatically

Our services automatically collect certain types of data when you interact with us. This includes log and usage data, device information, and location details, which help us understand how you use our services and improve your experience.

Information from Other Sources

In addition to the information you provide, we may also obtain data about you from other sources. This can include third-party services and public databases, which help us to enhance our records and better understand our customers.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used to provide, maintain, and continuously improve our services. We also use this data to personalize your experience, communicate with you about our services, and offer customer support.

Sharing of Information

Your personal information may be shared with third parties in certain circumstances. This includes sharing with service providers who act on our behalf, compliance with legal obligations, and protection of our rights and the safety of others.

Your Choices

You have control over your personal information. This includes choices regarding account information management, opting out of promotional communications, and setting preferences for cookies and similar tracking technologies.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, such as the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of the data we hold about you.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, or about how we handle your personal information, please don't hesitate to contact us at +1 (201) 200-9820. We are committed to working with you to obtain a fair resolution of any complaint or concern about privacy.
